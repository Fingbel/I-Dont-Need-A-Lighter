title=I Don't Need A Lighter
description=[h1] What is this mod ? [/h1]
description=[noparse] So you're telling me that people can't light a cigarette while sitting in front of a campfire or in a car running ? You can now light your cigarette from several sources of flames.[/noparse]
description=
description=[h2] Current Feature [/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*]  Light a cigarette when sitting in the front place of a vehicle. The key must be on the ignition or the vehicle must be hotwire. Battery must be functional and not empty
description=[*] Light a cigarette on Stove / Barbecue / Campfire / Wood Stove. The source must be on. Microwave is not a valid source.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]Mod Compatibility[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*]Smoker
description=[/list]
